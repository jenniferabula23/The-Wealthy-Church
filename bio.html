Romeo Eric Paakwasi Blankson was born on 17th February 1977 to Mr. Joseph Kwaku Blankson and Mrs. Rose Ewurakosua Blankson in Accra Nungua. He was born to a family of 6, he is the 5th born. He started his elementary school at St. Anthony Preparatory School, he had his second school education from O'Reilly Secondary school. Upon recognizing the call of God upon his life, he opted preparation for Ministry so he started his Theological Education from Hope institute of Theology where he has his Diploma in Theology, he also had his Bachelor of Arts Degree in Theology from New Covenant Seminary all in Accra respectively. After his first degree in 2002 he had the opportunity to work as the Church Administrator for Life Christian Center who transfered him to Kumasi to Pastor a Branch. He because a Bible College Lecturer at the Zion Bible College where He taught for 8 years before continuing his Master in Divinity Degree and PhD in Expository Communications from the Faith University in Florida USA respectively. By the Leading of the Spirit of God, he was led to start Ideal Voice Worship Centre-The Wealthy Church because of the specific instruction by the Spirit from 2Timothy 2:1-2 to impart others with CORRECT INTERPRETATION of the Scriptures. IVWC:The Wealthy Church was born in 4th September 2010. Dr. Romeo Eric PaaKwasi Blankson is married to an adorable woman of God Mrs. Flora Blankson and are bless with 3 outstanding children: Othniel Paa Kofi Blankson, Rozni Ewurakosua Blankson & Immanuel Nana Kwadwo Blankson They are a family of 5 with an exceptional grace for Ministry.
